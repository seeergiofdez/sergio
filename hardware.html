Hardware

This is a list of my personal/everyday devices.

  • Desktop: HP Prodesk 400 G6 Mini PC - i5-10500T, 16GB DDR4. Windows 10 Anniversary Update.
    • Performs surprisingly well for what I need lately. I don't play or do any heavy tasks, so this more than enough. It's super compact and supports USB-PD via USB C.
  • Laptop: HP EliteBook 840 G3 Mini PC - I5-6300U, 8GB DDR4. Windows 10 Anniversary Update.
    • I just use for watching some series or football in the TV. Rarely use it. Just does the job.
  • Phone: Refurbished iPhone 11 Pro. Stock OS.
    • Same thing, more than enough for what I have, just less than ten apps installed. Bought it September 2023, and hadn't changed it since. Battery lasts for two days straight.
  • Server: HP Prodesk 600 G2 Mini PC - i5-6500T, 8GB DDR4. Windows 10 Anniversary Update.
    • This is the mini PC used for my low-power DIY NAS. The hardware is literally overkilled for what I use, just to use it for storing files and getting to them over the local network. Connected via ethernet, set to sleep after some hours of inactivity.
