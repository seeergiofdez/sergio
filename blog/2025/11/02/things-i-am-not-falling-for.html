Things I'm NOT Falling For

Nov 2, 2025

Recently noticed a trend on social media called "propaganda I'm not falling for", where people list the trends they're consciously avoiding. Found it via secondhand exposure to social media stuff (mainly through organization's Slack), and found it really interesting.

So, I thought I'd join in. It's funny to share and get to know people via their answers.

Things I'm NOT falling for:

  • Needing social media
  • Having to always use the latest tech
  • Electric vehicles being more env-friendly
  • Being unable to make a difference
  • Having a strict year plan
  • Privacy pessimism
  • Hookup culture
  • Using web frameworks for a single website
  • Having to monetize your skills or hobbies
  • Remote work (I really need to physically distinguish from work and personal time)
  • Doing things by yourself being sad or boring
  • Romanticizing love from books/movies
  • AI chatbots

Propaganda I AM falling for:

  • Minimalism and digital minimalism
  • Hybrid vehicles
  • Waking up early
  • Coffee meetings
  • Private life, small circle
  • Accepting that not everything is under our control
  • Dolce far niente
  • Sleeping 9 hours
  • Conquering rooftops as the sun sets
